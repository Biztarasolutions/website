## Typography & Spacing

- **Font Family**: Inter (clean, modern, web-optimized)
- **Line Height**: 1.6 for readability
- **Font Weights**: 300-700 for hierarchy
- **Spacing**: 20px-80px padding, consistent gaps
- **Button Padding**: 12px 24px (accessible sizing)
- **Border Radius**: 8-16px (modern rounded corners)
